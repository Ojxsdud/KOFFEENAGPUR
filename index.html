Nagpur has a growing café scene that doesn't get nearly enough attention. This is an attempt to document it honestly — no sponsored posts, no PR visits, no inflated ratings. Just genuine visits and straightforward opinions.

Each review is posted first on Reddit's r/nagpur community, where locals can weigh in and share their own experiences. This site collects them in one place.

Scoring is out of 10. A 7 is genuinely good. A 5 means it's worth knowing about but come with tempered expectations. A 10 hasn't happened yet.
